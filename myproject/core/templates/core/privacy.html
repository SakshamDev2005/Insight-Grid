Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our educational website.

1. Information Collection

We do not collect personally identifiable information unless explicitly provided (e.g., through contact forms). The data you upload (like CSV files) is processed temporarily and not stored permanently on our servers.

2. Use of Data

Uploaded files and entered information are used solely to provide the requested data visualization or analysis. We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

3. Cookies and Analytics

We may use cookies or basic analytics to understand how users interact with the site and improve the learning experience. This does not include personal data.

4. Data Security

We implement basic security measures to protect your data during use. However, as this is an educational platform, we advise not uploading any sensitive or confidential data.

5. Policy Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Please review it periodically for any changes.
